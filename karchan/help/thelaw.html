The Law

(unless otherwise stated below, the offending player will receive warnings to seize and dissist breaking a rule before steps are taken)

  1. Thou art responsible for the actions of thyne character at all times!
    The actions of the characters are, and always will be, the responsibility of the persons that created them.

  2. Thou willt treat what deputies tell thee as being law!
    Their decisions are final, there is no parool or appeal possible. If you dislike how they rule, you have the right to stop playing this mud.

  3. Thou shalt not harass people!
    For those of you who don't know what harassing is, here's a definition:

    harassing = if people think that you are harassing them, then you are harassing them!

    Abusive words, slang, or any other kind of language with the purpose to harass and be hurtfull also fall under this header.

    If the offense is sufficiently severe this is grounds for IMMEDIATE BANISHMENT from the game!

  4. Thou shalt not try to break/hack into the MUD
    Attempting to break/hack into the MUD and failing also falls under this header. Attempting to break/hack into the MUD "just for fun" or "because I was bored" or "to see if I could" also fall under this header. Breaking this rule is grounds for IMMEDIATE BANISHMENT from the game!

  5. Thou shalt not cheat
    Cheating will not be tolerated. If you find a bug, report it, then do not use it. No multiple logins, multiple playings, or helping your own characters with other characters you own.

  6. Thou shalt not give out quest solutions.
    If a fellow player is stuck on a quest it is allowed to give him small hints. It is also allowed for multiple players who do not know the solution to combine their search in a particular quest.

  7. Thou shalt type Quit, every time thou leavest this great game.
    If you don't and try to log in at a later time, the computer will indicate that another session is still active. This is more a Guideline then a solid black-and-white rule.

  8. Ignorance of these here written and unwritten rules is no excuse.
    Changes to these rules are at the discretions of the creator. Make sure to keep updated.
