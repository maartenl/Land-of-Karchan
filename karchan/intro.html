Welcome

Welcome to one of the first Graphical Muds available on the Word Wide Web. Let me give you the crash course in playing the game.

  1. First enter the Homepage at location http://www.karchan.org/karchan (But I guess you already found that, never mind) and click on the golden dragon. You will enter the Main Page of the Land of Karchan.
  2. After that, in order to logon to the game for the very first time, click on LogonScreen.
  3. You will now get a page where you can fill out the name of your character in the game and a password. Make the name kinda original, and please write down your password somewhere.
  4. Click on the Submit button.
  5. There are two options.
    1. the name already exists, and you get a Wrong password error page. In this case you will have to think up a new name.
    2. the name does not exist, and you receive a registration form concerning details about you and your character.
  6. Click on the Submit button to enter the game. If you wish to leave the game, type Quit at the entry-field. Your character will be saved.
If you have any more questions, do not hesitate and feel free to ask.
