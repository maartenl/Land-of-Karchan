Help on Attributes

Attributes are specific values attached to individual instances of either rooms, items, itemdefinitions or characters. You are yourself allowed to set and get your own attributes for your own use, however the following attributes have significance in the game:
objectnamevaluevalue type
characteroccupationshopkeeperstring
itemdescriptionstring
item(container)isopenableboolean
item(container)isopenboolean
item(container)islockedboolean
item(container)capacityinteger
itemreadablestring
itemdrinkablestring
itemeatablestring
itemshopkeeperliststring, name of person, example: Karcas
